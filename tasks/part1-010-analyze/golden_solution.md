# Golden Solution - part1-010-analyze

The coupling capacitor applies the ac input to the gate, but the source of `M1` is shorted to ground. Therefore the device is not source-degenerated by a resistor or follower action; it is an ac common-source stage with a fixed source.

At midband, the gain is approximately

`Av ~= -gm (RD || ro)`

or `-gm RD` if `ro` is neglected. The capacitor only sets a low-frequency high-pass corner with the input resistance seen at the gate bias network.
