# Golden Solution - part1-011-many-poles-have

There are three independent energy-storage capacitors in Figure 8: `CGS`, `CGD`, and the drain/bulk or output capacitance `CDB`/`CSB` as connected in the small-signal model.

Equivalently, the circuit has energy storage associated with the input/source-side capacitance, the output/drain-side capacitance, and the bridging capacitance `CGD`. Thus the transfer function can have three poles in the full small-signal model. Counting should be based on independent capacitors and state variables, not merely on visible circuit nodes.
