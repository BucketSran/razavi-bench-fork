# Golden Solution - part1-014-cmos-inverter

Figure 11(a) is a CMOS inverter. The PMOS pull-up device connects the output to `VDD` when the input is low, and the NMOS pull-down device connects the output to ground when the input is high.

As an analog stage biased near its switching point, it behaves as a high-gain inverting amplifier with transconductance approximately `gmn + gmp` and output resistance `ron || rop` in small signal.
