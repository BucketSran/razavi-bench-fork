# Golden Solution - part2-004-double-widths-nmos-and-pmos-devices-phase

Doubling the widths of all NMOS and PMOS devices reduces the phase noise by about 3 dB.

A linear scaling argument gives this result: doubling device widths doubles the drive strength and capacitances in a way roughly equivalent to placing two identical ring oscillators in parallel. The signal power/current capability doubles relative to the uncorrelated device noise contribution, giving an approximately factor-of-two phase-noise improvement, or 3 dB.
